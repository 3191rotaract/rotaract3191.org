    About Us

    Know more About Us

    Rotaract District Organization 3191 comprises of Rotaract Clubs from Bangalore, Ramanagara, Kolar and Chittor. RIY 2023 - 2024 is the first year of RI District 3191. Rotaract 3191 is an ensemble of dynamic young minds determined to make a positive difference in society! Filled with frolic and fellowship, Rotaract has been a platform for thousands to come up with innovative solutions to positively impact the world, while we CONNECT, PARTNER & GROW together.

    Know More About Rotaract International

    Rotaract is a global youth service organization sponsored by Rotary International, aimed at young professionals and students between the ages of 18 and above. It offers opportunities for personal and professional development while engaging in community service.

    To join Rotaract, you can reach out to a local Rotaract club in your area and express your interest in becoming a member. Attend their meetings or events to get to know the club members and learn more about their activities.

    No, previous volunteer experience is not a requirement to join Rotaract. The organization welcomes individuals from diverse backgrounds and experiences who are passionate about making a positive impact in their communities.

    The frequency of meetings may vary among different Rotaract clubs. Typically, clubs meet either weekly, biweekly, or monthly, depending on their preferred schedule and availability of members.

    Yes, it is possible to be a member of both Rotaract and Rotary clubs. Many individuals transition from Rotaract to Rotary as they age out of the Rotaract age range or wish to further their involvement in service.

    Rotaract clubs undertake a wide range of service projects, including community development initiatives, environmental projects, health and wellness campaigns, educational programs, fundraising events, and more. The projects are aligned with the six causes of Rotaract and cater to the specific needs of their communities.

    Yes, Rotaract is an international organization with clubs in various countries around the world. It provides a platform for young adults to connect, collaborate, and engage in service on both a local and global scale.
