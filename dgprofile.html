RTN UDAYKUMAR K BHASKARA

Uday Kumar K Bhaskara is a charter member of the Rotary Club of Bangalore Cubbon Park and served as its eighth President in 2003-04. During his tenure, the club gained recognition for winning the 'Most Innovative Project' award for JAWS - Job Access with Speech software, benefiting visually impaired individuals in Bangalore and Karnataka. He pioneered the concept of KINDERACT Clubs, starting 20 clubs in two years for young children in Rotary District 3190.

Uday Kumar K Bhaskara has held vital roles in the district, including Assistant Governor, Director of International Service, Director of CSR, Board Meeting Coordinator, and District Deputy Trainer. He played a key role in introducing new initiatives during his time as the District Director for Club Service, particularly amid the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

A dedicated Rotarian, Uday Kumar K Bhaskara is a regular contributor and major donor to The Rotary Foundation. He has a background in the banking sector and is currently the Managing Partner of Rappers and Packers, a successful paper corrugated box manufacturing industry. He is also involved as a Director in QED Agro Systems Pvt Ltd, a company specializing in color sorters for the rice and pulses industry. A native of Bangalore, Uday Kumar K Bhaskara grew up with strong human values and the principles of peaceful coexistence in a joint family.
