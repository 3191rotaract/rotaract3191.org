About Me

A Rotaractor since 2015 and a Rotarian since 2015,Rotaract has been a significant aspect of my life for more than eight years, and it continues to be. During this period, I have held various leadership positions within the Rotary/Rotaract movement, both at the Club and District levels, contributing responsibly. This journey has instilled in me a greater sense of compassion, while also presenting me with countless opportunities for personal growth and networking, all the while making a positive impact on society. It has been a fulfilling and deeply satisfying journey, akin to finding friends who feel like family. One quote that resonates with me as a Rotaractor is: "Rotaraction is true and Rotaraction is inevitable."
