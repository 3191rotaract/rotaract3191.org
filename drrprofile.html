PP RTR RTN ARUN TEJA GODAVARTHI

Rtr. Arun Teja Godavarthi is a dedicated Rotaractor with a highly accomplished professional journey spanning over a decade. Throughout his Rotaract journey, Arun has consistently demonstrated exceptional leadership skills and a deep commitment to making a positive impact.

Arun's Rotaract journey began in 2015-2016 as a Charter Member and International Service Director of the Rotaract Club of CMRIT. His passion and dedication quickly propelled him to prominent leadership roles, including Secretary and President of the club during the Golden Jubilee Year of Rotaract. Arun is a Charter member of Rotaract Bangalore East and a Rotarian from Rotary Bangalore East. These experiences allowed Arun to leave an indelible mark on the organisation and inspire those around him.

Within the Rotaract District Council 3190, Arun has held various vital positions, showcasing his administrative prowess and transformative vision. From being a District Committee member to serving as District Web Services Director, District Joint Secretary, and ultimately District Rotaract Secretary for Operations & Training, Arun has consistently driven positive change and ensured the smooth functioning of the District.

Arun's exceptional contributions to the Rotaract movement have been recognized and celebrated with numerous awards. He has been honoured with the Outstanding District Secretary Award by SEARIC MDIO for two consecutive years, along with accolades such as Best Executive Council Member, Memorial Leadership Award, Young Achievers Award, and Outstanding District Training Plan. These accolades serve as a testament to his unwavering dedication and remarkable achievements.

Outside of Rotaract, Arun's professional journey has been equally impressive. He began as a Founding member of various ventures at a young age, displaying his entrepreneurial spirit and drive. Over time, he transitioned into the role of a Community Strategy Consultant, working with a diverse range of clients across the globe. Currently, Arun holds the role of "Developer Community Manager, India Developer Ecosystem" at Google, where he continues to make a significant impact by fostering vibrant developer communities.

Rtr. Arun Teja Godavarthi's multifaceted background, exceptional leadership skills, and relentless pursuit of excellence make him an inspiring role model for aspiring leaders and professionals. His passion for Rotaract and dedication to making a positive difference have left a lasting impact on the organisations he has been a part of. We applaud his achievements and eagerly anticipate his future endeavours as he continues to set new benchmarks and contribute to the betterment of society.
