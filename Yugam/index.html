About YUGAM

Welcome to YUGAM - A Step Towards A New Epoch, the first Rotaract District Conference of Rotaract District Organization 3191. YUGAM signifies not just a gathering, but a convergence of enthusiastic minds and dedicated hearts, united in the spirit of service and leadership. As we embark on this historic journey, we are poised to set new milestones and carve a legacy of excellence in service to humanity.
The conference will feature a mix of keynote speakers, talks, workshops, and networking sessions. The event is a great opportunity to learn from the best in the industry, meet fellow rotaractors, and grow your network.
