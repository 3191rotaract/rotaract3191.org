RTN B S SHANKAR

B S Shankar is a charter member of Rotary Bangalore Spandana, a Rotary Club established in 2014. Throughout his involvement in the club, he has held various significant roles including Club Service Director, Club Secretary, Membership Chair, and Club President in the year 2018-19.

At the district level, B S Shankar has actively served in multiple capacities. In the year 2019-20, he took charge as the District Interact Committee Chairman, followed by his role as Adult Literacy Chairman in 2020-21. In subsequent years, he served as the Director for Water and Sanitization in 2021-22 and Assistant Governor in 2022-23, demonstrating his commitment to community development.

Before his involvement in Rotary, B S Shankar pursued a career in the water treatment industry for over 16 years, holding various positions. In 2010, he embarked on an entrepreneurial journey by establishing Sankalp Enterprises, a company focused on providing high-quality water treatment solutions to pharmaceutical companies.

With his unwavering dedication to humanitarian services, B S Shankar continues to make a difference in the lives of individuals and communities, embodying the spirit of Rotary's motto: "Service Above Self."
