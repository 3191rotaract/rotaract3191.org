ಅನಿsutide – Club Hopping of DRRE Anirudh Kulkarni

ಅನಿsutide is more than just a visit — it’s a two-way dialogue between the DRRE and your club. The idea is simple: create a space where leaders and members can openly share experiences, challenges, and aspirations, while also gaining clarity on district priorities for the year

Why ಅನಿsutide?

  • Express yourself regarding the support you expect from the District
  • Brainstorm on what is good and what can be improved in both the District and Clubs
  • Initiate conversations about future mega-project ideas and how the District can assist Clubs in Fundraising
  • A platform to express concerns and work towards their solutions
  • Establish a bond to continue working together as Club & in harmony, to form a better Rotaract Movement

  • Know More About Me

    Rtn. Rtr. Anirudh G Kulkarni is a political strategist by profession and a Rotaractor by passion! Known for his dynamic leadership, sportsmanship, and commitment to service, he has been an integral part of the Rotaract movement since 2017.

    Born in Hubballi and raised in Bengaluru, Anirudh completed his schooling at The New Cambridge English High School and went on to earn his Bachelor of Commerce degree from Surana College, Bangalore University. A Rajya Puraskar Scout and national-level sportsman, he has proudly represented Karnataka in football, hockey, and floorball—experiences that shaped his resilience, teamwork, and leadership spirit.

    Anirudh began his Rotaract journey with the Rotaract Club of Surana College, later serving as President of the Rotaract Club of Banashankari. His growing influence led him to multiple district-level leadership roles, including District Sergeant-at-Arms, Zonal Rotaract Representative (for two consecutive years), Director for Public Image & Partnership and Outreach, and District Rotaract Secretary – Operations.

    He has chaired impactful initiatives such as the 1st Rotaract Friendship Cup and co-chaired Yugam, the first Annual Rotaract District Conference, both of which significantly boosted fellowship and visibility in the district. Currently, he continues his journey with the Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar, carrying forward his legacy of service and mentorship.

    Beyond Rotaract, Anirudh is a skilled political strategist with a strong grasp of grassroots politics and electoral campaigns. From interning at Asianet Suvarna News to working as a Sports Analyst at Hudl, his career has evolved into consulting with political leaders and parties across India to design data-driven campaigns and manage ground-level execution.

    A true Bengalurean at heart, he can often be found at the Kanteerava Stadium cheering for Bengaluru FC from the iconic West Block A or enjoying a game of tennis with friends. A leader by action, strategist by profession, and Rotaractor by passion—Rtn. Rtr. Anirudh G Kulkarni continues to inspire many with his dedication to service, leadership, and impact.
