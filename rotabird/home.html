    About Us

    Know more About Us

    Rotaract District Organization 3191 comprises of Rotaract Clubs from Bangalore, Ramanagara, Kolar and Chittor. RIY 2023 - 2024 is the first year of RI District 3191. Rotaract 3191 is an ensemble of dynamic young minds determined to make a positive difference in society! Filled with frolic and fellowship, Rotaract has been a platform for thousands to come up with innovative solutions to positively impact the world, while we CONNECT, PARTNER & GROW together..

    Rotaract District 3191's mission is to serve as a resource to Rotaract Clubs within our District by:

    • STRENGTHENING existing Rotaract Clubs within our District by providing resources and training.
    • ESTABLISHING new Rotaract Clubs in Namma Bengaluru.
    • CREATING opportunities for Rotaract Clubs to collaborate with Rotarians on projects, events, and fundraising initiatives.
    • BRIDGING the Rotary-Rotaract relationship at a District level.
    • REPRESENTING Rotaract Clubs within Rotary District 3191.

    Rotary Areas Of Focus

    Here are the Rotary Seven Areas of Focus

    The Rotary Foundation, a global organization dedicated to service and humanitarian work, focuses on seven key areas known as the Rotary Seven Areas of Focus. These areas encompass a range of sectors where Rotary members work to make a positive and lasting impact on local and international communities.

    Peacebuilding and Conflict Resolution

    Rotaract encourages conversations to foster understanding within and across cultures. We train adults and young leaders to prevent and mediate conflict and help refugees who have fled dangerous areas.

    Disease Prevention and Treatment

    Focusing on initiatives to prevent and control diseases, improve access to healthcare, and support medical research and interventions.

    Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH)

    Addressing water scarcity, improving sanitation facilities, and promoting hygiene practices to enhance public health and well-being.

    Maternal and Child Health

    Supporting programs and services that improve the health and well-being of mothers and children, including prenatal care, nutrition, immunizations, and access to healthcare.

    Basic Education and Literacy

    Promoting access to quality education, enhancing literacy skills, and supporting educational infrastructure and resources.

    Economic and Community Development

    Initiating projects and activities that stimulate economic growth, entrepreneurship, and sustainable development within communities, focusing on poverty alleviation and empowerment.

    Supporting the Environment

    Rotary recognizes the importance of environmental conservation and sustainability. Rotary members engage in activities such as tree planting, environmental education, waste management projects, and efforts to mitigate climate change.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Rotaract is a global youth service organization sponsored by Rotary International, aimed at young professionals and students between the ages of 18 and 30. It offers opportunities for personal and professional development while engaging in community service.

    To join Rotaract, you can reach out to a local Rotaract club in your area and express your interest in becoming a member. Attend their meetings or events to get to know the club members and learn more about their activities.

    No, previous volunteer experience is not a requirement to join Rotaract. The organization welcomes individuals from diverse backgrounds and experiences who are passionate about making a positive impact in their communities.

    The frequency of meetings may vary among different Rotaract clubs. Typically, clubs meet either weekly, biweekly, or monthly, depending on their preferred schedule and availability of members.

    Yes, it is possible to be a member of both Rotaract and Rotary clubs. Many individuals transition from Rotaract to Rotary as they age out of the Rotaract age range or wish to further their involvement in service.

    Rotaract clubs undertake a wide range of service projects, including community development initiatives, environmental projects, health and wellness campaigns, educational programs, fundraising events, and more. The projects are aligned with the six causes of Rotaract and cater to the specific needs of their communities.

    Yes, Rotaract is an international organization with clubs in various countries around the world. It provides a platform for young adults to connect, collaborate, and engage in service on both a local and global scale.
