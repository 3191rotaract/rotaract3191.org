RTN PRASANNA HEGDE

Rtn Prasanna Hegde, born on 12th December 1971 in Sirsi, Malenaadu, hails from a business family. He completed his Degree in Commerce and embarked on a remarkable journey in the world of Rotary and Rotaract. In 1994, he joined Rotaract in District 3170, and his dedication and passion for service led him to become the President of the Rotaract Club of Sirsi in 1997. Displaying exceptional leadership skills, he served as the District Rotaract Representative (DRR) in 1999-2000, leaving a lasting impact on the organisation.

Later, Prasanna Hegde relocated to Bangalore, where he became a charter member of the Rotary Bangalore Uttarahalli club. With his unwavering commitment, he held various positions within the club, contributing significantly to its growth and development. In recognition of his exceptional contributions, he was elected as the President of the club during the year 2019-2020, spearheading various impactful initiatives.

Professionally, Prasanna Hegde is a Working Partner in Chenda Impex n Decor LLP, a firm specialising in importing Wooden flooring and Pre-lam plywood. His business acumen and expertise in the field have contributed to the firm's success and growth.

Through his extensive involvement in Rotary and Rotaract, his leadership roles, and his professional accomplishments, Prasanna Hegde has emerged as a dedicated and influential individual, making a positive impact in his community and beyond.
